Artifact 1 – AI Lab Experience

Overview

In Workshop 1, I completed an introductory AI Lab activity designed to explore how artificial intelligence systems interact with users and generate responses. This activity helped me better understand how AI tools work and how they can be used for research, productivity, and problem solving.

Objective

  • Explore how AI tools generate responses
  • Understand the importance of prompts
  • Analyze the strengths and limitations of AI systems
  • Develop critical thinking when using AI technologies

Process

  • Accessed an AI tool and experimented with prompts
  • Asked different questions related to real-world topics
  • Observed how the AI generated responses
  • Evaluated the quality and usefulness of the information
  • Reflected on how AI can support learning and productivity

What I Learned

This activity taught me that AI tools rely heavily on clear instructions and prompts. It also showed me that while AI systems can provide helpful insights, they should be used carefully because outputs may sometimes contain incomplete or incorrect information.

Understanding how to effectively interact with AI tools is an important skill in modern technology fields, and this lab represents my first step into the world of AI and machine learning.

Reflection

Creating my first professional portfolio artifact helped me understand how important it is to present my work in a clear and professional way. Before this activity, I had not considered how documenting my learning experiences could demonstrate my skills and growth to potential employers or collaborators.

For this artifact, I chose to present my AI Lab experience from Workshop 1. Although it initially seemed like a simple learning exercise, I realized it represents an important step in my development in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

This process also helped me think about my audience. A professional portfolio should clearly communicate what I did, what I learned, and why the work is valuable. Explaining my experience helped me practice describing technical topics in a way that others can easily understand.

As I continue my studies, I plan to expand this portfolio with more projects that demonstrate my skills in AI, machine learning, and problem solving.
